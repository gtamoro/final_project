Welcome

Mac n Choux is a Melbourne based patisserie specialising in macarons and choux pastries. Our products are thoughtfully crafted using the finest quality ingredients and materials. But expect great pastries with our own twist! Founded in 2016, we (Nelly and Grace met at pastry school) came together over our love of all things pastry. Contact us for all your cake and dessert catering needs. Or say hi when we head out to some of Melbourne's best farmers markets.
